# Reviews + AI Visibility Bundle

> **Canonical pricing source:** [[AOH Ground Truth]]

## What it is

The primary upsell from Reviews Launch.

Bundle combines:
1. Reviews execution (email automation + response workflow + GBP operations)
2. AI visibility optimization (structured data/entity alignment and ongoing AI-search tuning)

## Price

- **$149/mo**
- Month-to-month
- No annual contract required

## How it is sold

- Position as the natural "step 2" after Reviews Launch starts proving value
- Can be sold direct when a prospect clearly asks to rank in AI/search now
- Use plain-language framing: "show up in ChatGPT and Google AI"

## What makes it different from Reviews Launch

Reviews Launch = review capture engine.

Bundle adds the **visibility layer**:
- on-site structured data and entity alignment (with client authorization)
- ongoing AI-search recommendation tuning
- monthly "where you are / where competitors are" visibility narrative

## Scope + permission rule

Hard rule:
- No hidden website edits.
- Any website technical work requires explicit client authorization.

Suggested onboarding language:
- "Authorize AOH to perform limited on-site visibility optimization required to improve search and AI recommendation coverage."

## Standalone AI Visibility policy

AI Visibility as a standalone offer is currently:
- **Call-only**
- Quoted after audit and access review

Reason:
- preserve delivery quality while fulfillment playbook is still maturing

**Active test exception (2026-05-08):**
- AOH is running a cold-email **$3/day AI Visibility** test lane.
- Treat as controlled experiment: use report + form + booked-call flow, and keep explicit permission for any site changes.

## Core sales talk track

> "Reviews gets you more fresh proof. AI Visibility gets that proof discovered when people ask ChatGPT or Google AI who to hire. Bundle gives you both for one monthly price."

## Upgrade trigger cues (when to pitch)

Pitch bundle when:
- client asks about ranking or "why competitors still show up first"
- review velocity improves but lead flow is still flat
- client wants "done-for-you" and does not want to touch technical setup
