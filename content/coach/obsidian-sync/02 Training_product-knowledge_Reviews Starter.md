# Reviews Launch (formerly Reviews Starter)

> **For canonical positioning, brand voice, and strategy facts, see [[AOH Ground Truth]].** This doc is the product-knowledge overview for Coach + reps — it pulls strategic context from Ground Truth and stays focused on the operational specifics of THIS offer.

---

## What it is

A done-for-you review-generation system. After every customer interaction, an automated workflow sends a personalized email asking for a Google review. Happy customers get a one-tap link to Google. Unhappy customers get routed to a private feedback form first, so issues land with the owner — not on the public review page.

Runs on hub360ai (our platform), configured by AOH, on autopilot.

## Strategy behind this offer — INTERNAL (Coach + reps only, NOT customer-facing)

Reviews Launch is **AOH's bulk-onboard low-barrier entry point.** $1/day for 30 days, then $99/mo is intentionally simple because:

1. **Goal is volume.** Get prospects into hub360ai cheaply. Upsell over time to Pro / Voice Agent / Lead Gen / Website.
2. **Google rewards review FREQUENCY > raw VOLUME.** A business with 1 fresh review/month + 10 total reviews ranks higher than one with 100 stale reviews. This means Reviews Starter wins for any business consistently sending us their customer list — even low-volume ones.
3. **Unit economics (internal):** Per-client onboarding ~30 min. After that, near-zero marginal cost — high-margin recurring at scale.
4. **Niche exclusion:** **Don't pitch Reviews Starter to auto repair shops in cold-email.** That niche is heavily saturated by Podium / Birdeye / similar tools — close rate is too low. Auto repair gets pitched other AOH offers (Voice Agent, Lead Gen) where AOH has differentiation.

## Who it's for

- Service businesses with at least one customer transaction per week (the trickle of review-asks compounds even at low volume per Google's frequency-weighted ranking)
- Owners with a Google Business Profile (or willing to set one up — we set up free during onboarding)
- Customers who balk at $200+/mo but will say yes to a low-friction $1/day start
- Any business that currently asks for reviews "when they remember" (i.e., almost no one)

**Revenue floor:** $300K annual revenue minimum (per Ground Truth tiered floor — Reviews Starter has the lowest floor of all SKUs).

## The "before" vs "after" state

Examples for cold-email and sales conversation context — not actual client data (AOH has zero clients yet).

**A typical "before" looks like:**
- Most recent Google review is months old
- Star rating hasn't moved in months
- Owner says "I'll ask my next customer" and forgets
- Competitors with hundreds of reviews show up first in local search

**A typical "after" should look like:**
- Steady trickle of fresh reviews every month, automatically
- Star rating climbing each quarter
- Most recent review is consistently within the last 7 days (recency = ranking signal per Google's local algorithm)
- Unhappy customers route to private feedback first — public review page stays clean
- Owner gets a weekly digest: "X reviews this week. Average Y stars. Z customer flagged a complaint — see internal note."

## What's included

**Onboarding — target: self-serve via walkthrough video (low-touch by design)**

Reviews Launch is the entry price-point ($1/day then $99/mo) — the onboarding model matches: clients should mostly self-serve so AOH labor stays minimal. Goal is "client signs up, watches video, system runs."

Once the walkthrough video is built (planned during engine + website phase), the flow is:

1. Client signs up + pays via Stripe
2. Welcome email arrives with link to walkthrough video
3. Client watches video and follows along:
   - Logs into hub360ai (their dashboard)
   - Connects Google Business Profile (client does this on their own screen — AOH never sees or handles passwords)
   - Connects socials at the same time (positions for future upsells, even if not active now)
   - Uploads customer list (or starts fresh from today's customers forward)
4. Automated workflow activates with client's data
5. Trickle cadence pre-configured — review-asks start going out within 24 hours
6. **Total client time:** ~15-30 minutes of video + self-setup

**Interim (until walkthrough video is recorded):** AOH does the same flow via a manual Zoom (~30-60 min, Mike or Kip leads). Same security principle — client shares screen and connects accounts themselves. Once the video is recorded, this manual Zoom step disappears for new clients.

**Behind the scenes (AOH does, client never sees):**

- Pre-templated workflow gets activated for each new client (no per-client custom build needed)
- Bad-review filter (1-3 star feedback routed to a private form before the public review page)
- Trickle pacing tuned per niche

**Ongoing (mostly hands-off by design):**

- Workflow runs automatically — no client action needed
- Weekly performance digest delivered to client's email automatically
- **End-of-month review call (~15-30 min)** — review numbers from the month, discuss what's working, surface natural upsell opportunities (Pro, Voice Agent, Lead Gen, Website)
- AOH handles any workflow or email-template adjustments behind the scenes (no client involvement needed)
- Client communication is mostly email — phone/Zoom only for the monthly review call

## What's NOT included (with rep responses if a prospect asks)

**Responding to reviews on Google**
- Not in Starter. Owner can knock these out in ~5 min/week, OR upsell to Reviews Pro (Pro includes AI-drafted responses opt-in).
- *If asked:* "Starter doesn't draft responses for you — that's our Reviews Pro tier. Pro's AI is trained on your voice and drafts every reply for you to approve with one tap. Most owners start with Starter, see results, then upgrade when responding becomes a chore."

**Multi-location management**
- Starter handles a single Google Business Profile only.
- *If asked:* "Starter is built for one location. If you've got multiple, let me know how many — we can spec a multi-location setup or look at Reviews Pro, which handles the rank-tracking piece per location."

**Posting reviews to other platforms (Yelp, Facebook, BBB)**
- Google only at this tier — Google is where local-search ranking impact actually lives.
- *If asked:* "We focus on Google because that's where local-rank impact comes from. Yelp, Facebook, and BBB matter less for ranking. If you really want all-platform coverage we can discuss it, but most clients see Google as the priority and stay with that."

**Local-rank tracking (Google Maps visibility report — also called a heatmap in the industry)**
- That's Reviews Pro. Starter shows you review counts and ratings; Pro shows you where you rank in Google Maps.
- *If asked:* "Starter tells you how reviews are growing. Reviews Pro adds a Google Maps visibility report that shows exactly where you appear across your service area — green where you win, red where you don't. If knowing your rank matters, Pro's the upgrade — we can talk about that."

**Cold outreach to past customers (i.e., people you haven't served recently)**
- We work from the client's recent customer list (last 60-90 days), not a bought marketing list.
- *If asked:* "We don't blast — that gets you flagged as spam and can tank your sender reputation. We start with customers you've actually served in the last 60-90 days while the experience is fresh, and we build forward from there. It compounds — every new customer gets asked once."

## Common questions

**"How long until I see results?"**
Industry norms suggest first reviews within 7-14 days of going live. Steady cadence (a few per month) typically by week 4. Compounding effects on rank/visibility take 60-90 days. AOH will publish verified numbers once we have client data.

**"Do I qualify if my revenue is only $X?"**
Reviews Launch starts at $1/day for 30 days, then $99/mo. We typically work with businesses doing $300K+ in annual revenue — at that size, $99/mo is comfortable.

**"I don't have a Google Business Profile yet."**
No problem — setting one up is part of onboarding, free, takes about 15 minutes during your walkthrough. You'd be surprised how many local businesses skip this; it's the single biggest local-SEO move you can make and we handle it.

**"I only get a few customers per week — will this even work?"**
Yes, actually. Google rewards review FREQUENCY over total volume — a business with 1 fresh review per month + 10 reviews ranks higher than one with 100 stale reviews and nothing recent. Your low volume isn't a problem, it's exactly the kind of business this works well for.

**"What if a customer leaves a bad review anyway?"**
Our filter routes 1-3 star feedback to a private form first. Customers who insist on posting publicly will, but the filter catches roughly 80% of complaints before they go public. We don't censor — we redirect.

**"Will this work for my industry?"**
Mechanics are universal: post-service email → review link → filter. Niche-specific copy adjustments are baked into setup (funeral homes don't ask the same way as auto repair shops). For some industries that already have heavy review-tool competition, AOH leads with a different offer in cold outreach — but Reviews Starter still works mechanically if the prospect asks for it directly.

**"What if I already use [Birdeye / Podium / Reputation.com]?"**
Those tools cost $300-500/mo and require login + manual nudging. We're a fraction of the price, fully managed (no logging in to dashboards). If you already love your existing tool, stay. If you're paying for something you don't use, switch.

**"I have 10,000 past customers. Can you blast them all?"**
No — that would tank your sender reputation and trigger spam filters. We start with the last 60-90 days of customers (recent enough to remember the service) and build forward, with the trickle cadence configured during setup.
