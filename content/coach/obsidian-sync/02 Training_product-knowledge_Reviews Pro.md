## What it is

A done-for-you review-management system that handles SMS + email review-asks, AI-drafted review responses, negative-review filtering, Google Business Profile optimization, and monthly local-SEO reporting.

The Pro tier moves the client from "we get reviews" (what Starter does) to "we manage our entire review presence and rank in local search."

Runs on hub360ai (our platform), configured by AOH, on autopilot. Includes SMS-carrier registration — AOH handles the carrier compliance paperwork on the client's behalf so SMS review-asks are deliverable.

## How Pro differs from Starter

| | Starter ($99) | Pro ($199 + $199 setup) |

## What's included

### Onboarding — 3-phase flow (low-touch by design)

Same self-serve philosophy as Reviews Starter, with one focused 15-20 min Zoom for regulatory + sensitive items. Pro is sales-led to close, but the actual setup mostly mirrors Starter once the client signs.

**Phase 1 — Welcome email (auto, immediately after signup)**

Client closes on the sales call → signs up + pays ($199/mo + $199 setup; intro pricing per Mike's discretion if applicable). Within minutes:

- Welcome email arrives with **walkthrough video link** + **intake form link**
- Email also flags: "We'll need ~15-20 min on Zoom after you finish the form to capture a couple of regulatory items — schedule that here when ready: [Calendly link]"

**Phase 2 — Client self-serve (~30-45 min, on their own time)**

Client follows the walkthrough video and completes the intake form:

1. Logs into hub360ai (their dashboard)
2. Connects Google Business Profile (client does this on their own screen — AOH never sees or handles passwords)
3. Connects socials (positions for future upsells, even if not active now)
4. Uploads customer list (or starts fresh from today's customers forward)
5. Pastes 5-10 past review replies into the intake form for AI tone-matching (opt-in — clients who want full control of their voice can skip this)
6. Fills out intake form: business legal name, address, sample SMS messages (we provide templates), and other info AOH needs for SMS-carrier registration

**Phase 3 — Welcome Zoom (~15-20 min, scheduled by client after Phase 2)**

Short, focused, only on what self-serve CAN'T do:

- AOH confirms self-serve steps actually got done (visual check on screen share)
- **Confirm EIN verbally** (sensitive — never email/text)
- **Capture website credentials** via 1Password Teams shared vault (AOH needs this for AI-search optimization + GBP keyword work)
- Q&A
- Schedule next monthly review call

**After Zoom (AOH does, all in background):**

- Submits SMS-carrier registration via hub360ai's compliance flow (Fast Track ~$3, ~3 business days)
- Adds AOH as Editor/Admin collaborator on client's website (or uses Google Tag Manager workspace as fallback) — never via password sharing
- Builds the automated workflow in hub360ai
- Adds AI-search optimization to the website (schema markup + structured content so ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, etc. start citing the business)
- Trains AI response drafter on client's voice samples (if opted in)
- Sets up GBP optimization schedule (photos, posts, Q&A, descriptions — monthly)

**Live timing:**

- **Email review-asks go live Day 1** (don't wait for SMS approval to start delivering value)
- **SMS unlocks Day 5-10** after carrier approval (Fast Track via hub360ai's compliance flow)
- **AI-search optimization** starts surfacing in 30-60 days (AI engines need crawl cycles to update)

### Ongoing (mostly hands-off)

- SMS + email review-asks trickle automatically to recent customers (last 60-90 days, then forward)
- AI drafts a personalized response to every new Google review (if opted in; owner approves with one tap)
- AOH posts to GBP weekly (photos, updates, offers, Q&A)
- Monthly Google Maps visibility report delivered to client's email automatically
- **Monthly review call (~15-30 min)** — review rank movement, review velocity, AI-search citations, surface upsell opportunities (Voice Agent, Lead Gen, Website Rebuild)
- Quarterly formal local-rank report

## Common questions

**"How is this different from a real SEO agency?"**
Real SEO agencies charge $1,500-5,000/mo and focus on backlinks, technical SEO, and content marketing. Reviews Pro focuses on the local pack + AI search — the two places SMB customers actually find local services. We're a fraction of the cost and cover the high-leverage local-SEO outcomes.

**"Will my business show up when people ask ChatGPT or Google's AI for the best [vet/groomer/etc.] near them?"**
Right now, probably not — most local businesses don't. We make sure your business gets cited when prospects ask AI engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) local-search questions. By 2026-2027 this is replacing 30%+ of Google search for high-intent local queries. Most agencies aren't doing this yet — get in early. (Industry term for this is "AEO/GEO" — Answer Engine Optimization / Generative Engine Optimization — you may hear other vendors mention it.)

**"Why is SMS better than email for review asks?"**
Industry data shows SMS achieves roughly 3x higher review-ask response rates than email. People read texts within minutes; review-ask emails often get buried. SMS is the bigger reason Pro is worth the upgrade from Starter.

**"Why is there a wait before my SMS reviews start going out?"**
The wait is for SMS-carrier compliance approval. Every US business sending automated SMS has to register with the major carriers (T-Mobile, Verizon, AT&T) before they'll deliver your messages. It's mandatory and AOH handles the entire registration on your behalf during onboarding. Without it, carriers block your messages and your customers never get them. With our Fast Track process you're typically live in 5-10 business days. **Email review-asks go out from Day 1** — you don't have to wait for SMS to start collecting reviews. (Industry term for this is "A2P 10DLC" — you may hear other vendors mention it.)

**"Do I really need someone responding to reviews?"**
A response to every review (good or bad) signals to Google AND prospective customers that you're engaged. Industry data (BrightLocal): businesses that respond to 100% of reviews rank ~10-15% higher in local pack on average. The owner doing it manually is the #1 reason it doesn't get done.

**"How long until I see ranking improvements?"**
First Google Maps visibility report shows baseline at end of month 1. Movement starts in month 2-3. Industry norms suggest meaningful re-ranking (3-5 positions) by month 6 in most cases. Real client data will set our verified numbers as we accumulate it.

**"What if my Google Maps visibility is already good?"**
Then the report becomes a defensive monitoring tool — when a competitor enters your area, you see them on the map, you respond. Better than waiting until they've taken your customers.

**"Can I bundle Pro with other AOH offers?"**
Yes — the most common pair is **Pro + Website Rebuild as our "AI-Ready Local Stack"** (we waive the $199 Pro setup fee if you sign for both on Day 1, saves $199 upfront). Pro + Voice Agent gives you the full inbound stack (reviews + missed-call AI receptionist). Pro + AI Lead Gen adds the outbound prospecting layer. Most clients start with Pro alone and add layers at month 3-6.

**"Do I qualify if my revenue is only $X?"**
Reviews Pro is $199/mo plus a $199 setup. We typically work with businesses doing $300K+ annual revenue — at that size the math is comfortable. If you're below that, Reviews Starter at $79 might be a better fit; we can grow you into Pro later when the numbers justify it.

**"I don't have a Google Business Profile yet."**
No problem — setting one up is part of onboarding, free, takes about 15 minutes. Pro can't function without it though, so this is one of the first things we'll get done.

**"I'm worried about being one of your first clients."**
Honest answer — you'd be one of our first locally, and that's exactly why the price is what it is. We're not a 100-client agency yet; we're hands-on, you'd have direct access to me [Mike] whenever you need it. Most agencies don't offer that combination once they scale.
