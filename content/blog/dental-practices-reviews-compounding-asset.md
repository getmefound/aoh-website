## Reviews keep working after the appointment is over

A dental patient may stay with a practice for years. That makes trust expensive to lose and valuable to build.

Most practices know reviews matter. The part many miss is that reviews do not work like ads. Ads stop when the spend stops. Reviews keep sitting on your Google profile, helping the next nervous patient decide whether to call.

A review about a patient who felt heard, a hygienist who explained the visit, or a dentist who helped with anxiety can sell your practice better than any polished paragraph on your website.

That is why reviews compound. One review helps the next patient. Then the next. Then the next.

## The problem is not usually bad care

Most dental practices with weak review profiles are not bad practices. They just stopped asking.

The pattern is common:

- The practice got a wave of early reviews
- The front desk got busy
- The ask became inconsistent
- Months passed with nothing new
- A newer competitor started looking more active

Patients notice dates. They want to know what the practice is like now, not five years ago.

AI search and Google AI look for that same freshness. They are not only checking star rating. They are checking whether the Google Business Profile is complete, whether reviews are recent, and whether the website and Google listing tell the same story.

That means a dental practice with a solid score but stale reviews can lose attention to a practice that looks more current and easier to recommend.

## Google AI wants a clear practice profile

For a dentist, the trust picture has to be clean.

Google AI needs to understand:

- What services you offer
- Where the practice is located
- Whether hours and phone number are accurate
- Whether the website matches the Google listing
- Whether patients are leaving recent reviews
- Whether the practice replies like it is paying attention

Google uses relevance, distance, and prominence for local businesses. Google profile signals, website signals, and review signals are high-value factors for AI search.

Plain English: if the practice looks complete, active, and consistent, it is easier for AI search to recommend.
