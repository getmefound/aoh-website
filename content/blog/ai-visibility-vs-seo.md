## The short version

Old search work was about getting a link seen.

AI visibility is about getting your business recommended by name.

That is the shift. A customer does not have to click ten links anymore. They can ask ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, or Google AI, "Who should I call near me?" The answer may name one to three businesses. If your business is not in that answer, the customer may never see you.

That is a different game.

## AI search checks the whole business

AI search does not only look at your homepage. It looks for a clear picture of your business across the web.

It wants to know:

- What you do
- Where you work
- Whether your Google listing is complete
- Whether your website says the same thing as your Google listing
- Whether customers are still reviewing you
- Whether other trusted places list your business correctly

If those pieces do not match, AI search gets less confident. Less confidence means someone else gets recommended.

The strategy behind GetMeFound is built on that exact problem. Most local businesses are not broken. They are unclear to Google AI.

## What changed for local businesses

Google still matters. Your Google Business Profile still matters. Reviews still matter. Your website still matters.

What changed is how those pieces are being used.

Google AI now decides who to recommend before many customers ever click. BrightLocal 2026 data in our strategy says 45% of consumers now use AI for local recommendations, up from 6% one year ago. That is not a small behavior change. That is the market moving.

AI search rewards businesses that look complete, active, and consistent. It is not enough to have a nice website if your Google listing is thin. It is not enough to have old reviews if nothing fresh has happened in months. It is not enough to have a phone number on one page and a different one somewhere else.

## The work, in plain English

For a local business, AI visibility means:

- Your Google profile is filled out and accurate
- Your website matches your Google profile
- Your reviews are recent and responded to
- Your business appears correctly in trusted directory listings
- Your services and service area are easy to understand
- Your profile keeps showing activity over time

That is the part most owners miss. Getting found once is not enough. Google rewards businesses that stay active. The moment you go quiet, competitors can look more current.
